Events/Talks

As a Blockchain Evangalist, you'll often see me at universities and educational events speaking about the how and why of distributed ledger technologies. I cover topics from simple Blockchain 101s to indepth usecase exploration and technical deep dives into fringe areas of what's currently out where.

If you're interested in scheduling a talk, feel free to reach out to be via email (dev@blockchainspaceman) and see if I'm free. Any general educational workshops I do for free, but if you'd like to schedule a deep dive consultation for your company, I do have consulting rates available as well.

Projects

My projects range from simple hackathon project I'm proud of to paid development work we have accomplished with our clients.

  • Qmix
    This is project built by Kelvin Fichter for Spaceman Holdings LLC. We worked with our client, Qtum, to drastically reduce the steps to deploy smart contracts on their platform. Taking inspiration from Remix for Ethereum, we worked on a solution from the ground up to connect to a developer docker instance for Qtum and allow for deploying, accessing the deployed contract, and debugging right in browser. We then took the application to hackathons to help developers learn to use it and provide feedback.
    Check it out here
  • DistributedID
    Starting out as a hackathon project for Distributed: Trade in 2016, this project was a novel approach to the self soverign identity problem and not only won my team the hackathon, but encourged by VC interest, led to my first company. DIID is now situated in Toronto, Canada and works to solve fraud in the financial services market. You can check out the original devpost I worked on here
  • DREX
    Another Distributed: Trade winner, this time at Dist:Trade 2017, my team and I worked on a solution to better crowd fund critical pay-go solar infrastructure in Africa. This was specifcally a an answer to consulting prompt given to me by Impact Labs, a company working to solve this problem with the World Bank. It's been given to the World Bank as a proof of concept for their approaches in securitzing energy infrastruce in Tanzania. You can check it out here

About

Dev is Blockchain Solutions Architect based out of Chicago. He is currently working on getting his Bachelors at the Illinois Institute of Technology while working full time as a consultant with his company, Spaceman Holdings LLC. Spaceman Holdings works with a wide range of clients to provide everything from business development services, corporate education, developer relations and development outsourcing services in the distributed ledger industry. You can often find him touring the country in his iconic spacesuit giving talks and mentoring students on how to get started playing with distributed ledger technologies.

Dev discovered Bitcoin and blockchains back in 2013 when he argued his AP English teacher into letting him research cryptocurrencies for a paper. Delving into the world of the technology as a broke high school student, he decided to work on filling his brain with as much information as he could. Over the course of the last few years, he's built quite a win record at hackathons working on innovate and novel solutions using different blockchain frameworks, even building one of these into a company --> DistributedID.

Eventually leaving DistributedID to persue his own endevours in Chicago, he worked to build Spaceman Holdings, an LLC focused on tackling a wide vareity of different problems in the industry.

When not working on distributed ledger technologies Dev loves to fly drones, dance to Electroswing, cook, and explore his City (Chicago).
